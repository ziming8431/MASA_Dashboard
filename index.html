The protection gap is the bridge from climate risk to reinsurance opportunity. Public multi-year insured-claims data are not consistently available for both countries, so this uses EM-DAT economic losses with a 90% uninsured benchmark as a strategic screen — not a treaty-pricing model.

Takeaway: Material realised loss evidence + a structurally high uninsured share = a reinsurance opportunity. The gap is where climate risk becomes a balance-sheet problem.
